Where Friends Meet

A neighborhood institution on Route 73

For decades, The Jug Handle Inn has been the spot in Cinnaminson where game days, first dates and Friday nights all end up. Nationally famous wings, an 8 oz Angus burger built to order, and a bar that always has room for one more.

Featured on Food Network’s The Best Thing I Ever Ate and crowned a Best of Philly winner — but we’d rather just save you a stool.
