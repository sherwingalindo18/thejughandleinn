Jug Handle Laws

You’re home when you’re here. A few house rules, with love.

  • The restaurant is run for the enjoyment of our guests — if something’s not right, let us fix it.
  • Beef is tasty because it has some fat. Want lean? Order the turkey.
  • Our portions — especially salads, sandwiches and fries — are excessive, because nothing succeeds like excess.
  • We’re serious about food, but not much else. Relax and have a good time!
  • Please don’t drink and drive. It’s the other thing we take seriously at The Jug.

Thank you — Kevin & Nichole
