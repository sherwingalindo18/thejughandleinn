About The Jug

Since day one on Route 73

A Cinnaminson original

The Jug Handle Inn has been the heart of the neighborhood for decades — nationally famous wings, an ice-cold bar, and a welcome that turns first-timers into regulars.

We’re serious about the food and not much else. Come hungry, stay a while, and make yourself at home.

Our Story

What started as a neighborhood corner bar on Route 73 grew into one of South Jersey’s most-loved spots — without ever losing the feel of your local. Kevin and Nichole have spent years perfecting the wings, the welcome and the room full of regulars who feel like family.

The Tradition

Our wings originated in Buffalo and were perfected right here in Cinnaminson — lightly breaded, made fresh to order, and tossed in any of 20-plus house sauces and rubs. They earned us a spot on Food Network’s The Best Thing I Ever Ate and a Best of Philly nod, and they keep people driving in from all over.

Community

We’re a gathering place first. Game days, birthdays, fundraisers, after-work pints and Friday nights with live music on the Creekside — The Jug is where Cinnaminson comes together. You’re home when you’re here.

Food Philosophy

Everything is made fresh to order, because good food takes a minute and it’s worth the wait. Certified Angus burgers, hand-breaded wings, slow-smoked ribs and pulled pork, scratch dips and homemade desserts — generous portions, no shortcuts.

Hospitality

The room runs for the enjoyment of our guests, not the convenience of the staff. If something isn’t right, we want to fix it. Relax, settle in, and let us take care of you.

Why guests love us

Real food, real people, real good times

Award-winning wings, a kitchen that never cuts corners, and a staff that treats you like a neighbor. That’s the whole recipe.
